You can zoom in and out to adjust the size of the entire Bokuchi interface.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Action | macOS | Windows / Linux |
|--------|-------|-----------------|
| Zoom in | Cmd+Plus (=) | Ctrl+Plus (=) |
| Zoom out | Cmd+Minus | Ctrl+Minus |
| Reset zoom | Cmd+0 | Ctrl+0 |

## Status Bar

The current zoom level is displayed in the status bar at the bottom of the window. You can also click the zoom buttons there to adjust the level.

<img src={imgZoom.src} alt="Status bar showing zoom level with zoom in, zoom out, and reset buttons" width="500" />

## Zoom Range

- **Minimum:** 50%
- **Maximum:** 300%
- **Step:** 10% per increment
- **Default:** 100%

## Persistence

Your zoom level is saved automatically and restored when you reopen Bokuchi.
