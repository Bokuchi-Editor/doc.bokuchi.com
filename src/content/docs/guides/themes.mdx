Bokuchi comes with 9 built-in themes to match your preference.

## Available Themes

| Theme | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Default** | Light | Clean light theme with blue accents (Material Design) |
| **Dark** | Dark | Dark background with light text, easy on the eyes |
| **Pastel** | Light | Soft, gentle colors with mint green and pink accents |
| **Vivid** | Light | Bold, vibrant colors with orange and purple accents |
| **Dawn** | Light | Warm, soft light theme with a gentle terracotta tint, like a sunrise |
| **Twilight** | Dark | Dark theme with warm, golden tones, like a sunset |
| **Silk** | Light | Refined light monotone with a faint cool tint |
| **Ink** | Dark | Calm dark monotone with a faint cool tint |
| **Darcula** | Dark | Inspired by JetBrains IDEs, dark charcoal with warm syntax colors |

## Changing the Theme

There are two ways to switch themes:

### From Settings

1. Open **Settings** (Cmd+, on macOS / Ctrl+, on Windows/Linux)
2. Go to the **Appearance** section
3. Select your preferred theme

### From the Status Bar

Click the **theme name** displayed in the status bar at the bottom of the window. This provides a quick way to cycle through themes.

## Theme Persistence

Your theme choice is saved automatically and will be restored the next time you open Bokuchi.
