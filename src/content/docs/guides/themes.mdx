Bokuchi comes with 5 built-in themes to match your preference.

## Available Themes

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Default** | Clean light theme with blue accents (Material Design) |
| **Dark** | Dark background with light text, easy on the eyes |
| **Pastel** | Soft, gentle colors with mint green and pink accents |
| **Vivid** | Bold, vibrant colors with orange and purple accents |
| **Darcula** | Inspired by JetBrains IDEs, dark charcoal with warm syntax colors |

## Changing the Theme

There are two ways to switch themes:

### From Settings

1. Open **Settings** (Cmd+, on macOS / Ctrl+, on Windows/Linux)
2. Go to the **Appearance** section
3. Select your preferred theme

### From the Status Bar

Click the **theme name** displayed in the status bar at the bottom of the window. This provides a quick way to cycle through themes.

## Theme Persistence

Your theme choice is saved automatically and will be restored the next time you open Bokuchi.
