## Why Bokuchi?

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Completely Offline" icon="laptop">
    No internet connection needed. Your documents stay on your computer — no cloud, no account, no tracking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-Time Preview" icon="document">
    See your Markdown rendered instantly as you type. Split view, editor-only, or preview-only — your choice.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Tab Editing" icon="list-format">
    Open and manage multiple files at once with tabs. Drag and drop to reorder, and your session is automatically restored.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Powerful Features" icon="setting">
    Variables, table conversion, folder explorer, outline panel, 5 themes, 14 UI languages, and more — all built in.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
