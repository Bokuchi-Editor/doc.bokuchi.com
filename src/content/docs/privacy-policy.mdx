## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Bokuchi User Guide website (`doc.bokuchi.com`) collects and uses information from visitors.

## Use of Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects information such as:

- Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
- Your approximate geographic location (country/region level)
- Your browser type, operating system, and device category
- How you arrived at this website (referral source)
- Your interactions with the site (clicks, scrolls)

This data is collected through cookies and similar technologies provided by Google. The information is processed in aggregate to help us improve the documentation.

### What we do NOT collect

- Personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number
- Precise location data
- Any data you enter into the Bokuchi application

## Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies on your browser to distinguish unique users and track sessions. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

- Installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Disabling cookies in your browser settings

## Data Sharing

The analytics data collected is used solely to improve this documentation site. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by Google Analytics' data processing terms.

For details on how Google processes this data, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## About the Bokuchi Application

This privacy policy applies only to the `doc.bokuchi.com` website. The Bokuchi desktop application itself does not collect any user data or analytics.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Bokuchi-Editor/doc.bokuchi.com).
